

# Gutama Home Improvement — Premium Website

## Design System
- Dark luxury palette: Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1A), Slate Black (#111111), Bold Red (#C61E1E), Gold (#D4AF37), White, Light Gray (#F4F4F4)
- Google Fonts: Playfair Display for headings, Inter for body text
- Glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll animations, gold accent dividers
- Fully responsive with mobile sticky call button

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Landing Page (Homepage)
- **Hero**: Full-width cinematic background with dark overlay, gold/red glow accents. Headline: "Premium Roofing & Exterior Solutions Built to Last". Two CTAs: "Get Free Estimate" (red) + "Call Now: 973-820-5130"
- **Services Grid**: 11 service cards with icons, hover animations, and "Learn More" links (Roof Removal, Re-Roofing, Flat Roof, Slate Roof, Chimney, Siding, Carpentry, Additions, Dormers, Gutters, Emergency Repair)
- **Why Choose Us**: Icon cards — Licensed & Insured, Quality Guaranteed, Emergency Repair, Free Estimates, English & Español
- **Before & After**: Image comparison slider (3 transformations)
- **Service Areas**: Elegant grid of 19 Essex County municipalities with subtle NJ map background
- **Testimonials**: 3 realistic review cards with 5-star ratings
- **CTA Section**: Dark background with red gradient, phone numbers, bilingual support note

### 2. Shared Layout
- Premium navbar with logo, nav links, and "Get Free Estimate" CTA button
- Footer with contact info, quick links, service links, and social media placeholders

### 3. Individual Service Pages (11 pages)
Each page follows the same premium template:
- Hero section with service-specific headline
- Service description and process breakdown
- Why choose Gutama section
- FAQ accordion
- Bottom CTA

Services: Roof Removal, Re-Roofing, Flat Roof, Slate Roof, Chimney, Siding, Carpentry, Additions, Dormers, Gutters, Emergency Repair

### 4. Gallery Page
- Category filter tabs (All / Roofing / Siding / Additions / Chimney)
- Masonry grid layout with hover zoom effects
- AI-generated project showcase images

### 5. About Page
- Company story section
- Mission & vision
- Professional crew showcase
- Licensed & Insured badge highlight

### 6. Contact Page
- Contact form with service selection dropdown
- Phone numbers (English: 973-820-5130, Español: 973-342-4134)

### 7. Animations & UX
- Scroll-triggered fade-in and reveal animations

### Image Approach
- All images will use placeholder URLs initially, with AI-generated images to be created via the Lovable AI image generation API as a follow-up step (generating 30+ images in one pass would be impractical — we'll use high-quality placeholder compositions first, then replace iteratively)
